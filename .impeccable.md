## Design Context

### Users
Students, learners, freshers, and job seekers exploring AI upskilling and career growth. They often browse on mobile and want a guided, confidence-building experience that helps them discover what to learn next.

### Brand Personality
Ambitious, world-class, empowering. The interface should feel like the world's best learning experience: polished, clear, high-trust, and energizing without feeling overwhelming.

### Aesthetic Direction
Keep the current light-first educational product aesthetic, but make mobile interactions feel more refined and intentional. Swipeable cards are appropriate on mobile as long as they remain easy to scan, easy to tap, and visually breathable.

### Design Principles
1. Mobile should feel premium, not compressed.
2. Learning choices should be quick to scan and easy to compare.
3. Touch interactions should feel obvious and forgiving.
4. Preserve clarity and confidence over visual density.
5. Keep desktop structure stable while improving small-screen usability.
